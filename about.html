About Padheyam

Padheyam is a community-driven initiative aimed at reducing food waste and hunger. We provide a platform for individuals and businesses to donate surplus food, which is then distributed to NGOs and people in need.

Why It Matters

Millions go hungry every day while tons of food go to waste. FoodShare bridges this gap by making food sharing easy, safe, and impactful.

How It Works

  • Donors submit food details via our platform.
  • NGOs and individuals request food based on availability.
  • We facilitate safe and timely delivery.
